About Milan

Your Dream Venue
in Sikandra

◆

Milan Marriage Hall & Hotel (Ankita Tent House) is Sikandra's most sought-after venue for weddings, receptions, and celebrations. Situated at Mission Chowk on Lachhuar Road, we are conveniently located at the heart of Sikandra, Jamui.

From grand shaadis and vibrant receptions to intimate birthday parties, corporate meetings, ring ceremonies and anniversary celebrations — our beautifully maintained hall, elegant mandap, and dedicated staff ensure every occasion is unforgettable.

We offer both AC and Non-AC rooms for guest stay, ample parking, and professional event management — all at transparent, affordable prices.
